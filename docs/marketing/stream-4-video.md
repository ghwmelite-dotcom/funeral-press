## 4G. Production Checklist — Zero Budget Setup

### Equipment (Use What You Have)

| Item | Free Option |
|------|-------------|
| Camera | Smartphone (any model from 2021+) |
| Microphone | Phone's built-in mic (record in a quiet room) or wired earphone mic |
| Lighting | Natural window light (face the window) or a desk lamp |
| Tripod | Stack of books or lean phone against a water bottle |
| Screen recording | Built-in screen recorder (iOS/Android) or OBS (desktop, free) |
| Video editing | CapCut (free, mobile + desktop) or DaVinci Resolve (free, desktop) |
| Thumbnails | Canva (free tier) |
| Music | YouTube Audio Library (free, royalty-free) or CapCut built-in music |
| Graphics/text overlays | CapCut or Canva |
| Teleprompter | BigVu (free tier) or tape script to wall behind camera |

### Quality Checklist (Before Publishing)

- [ ] Audio is clear — no echo, no background noise
- [ ] Text on screen is large enough to read on a phone
- [ ] Branding: FuneralPress URL visible in first and last 3 seconds
- [ ] Thumbnail is readable at small size (phone feed)
- [ ] Caption/description includes target keywords
- [ ] Hashtags included (5-10 per post)
- [ ] CTA is clear and specific
- [ ] Video is the correct aspect ratio (9:16 for Shorts/Reels/TikTok, 16:9 for YouTube)
- [ ] No copyrighted music — use royalty-free only
- [ ] Captions/subtitles added (many viewers watch on mute)
