# Pomelli Campaigns + Product Imagery

Ready-to-use marketing kit for **Google Pomelli** (Google Labs AI marketing tool) and any social channel. Contains: the manual brand setup, six paste-ready campaign prompts, and the matching product hero images (square + vertical) hosted on R2.

> **Why manual setup:** Pomelli's "scan a URL" flow expects a single e-commerce product page and rejects funeralpress.org (a multi-product SaaS platform) with *"A product could not be extracted from this URL."* This is **not** a Cloudflare/robots block — it's a structural mismatch. Use Pomelli's **"enter brand manually / skip scan"** option and paste the Brand DNA below.

---

## Brand DNA (paste into Pomelli's manual brand setup)

- **Brand name:** FuneralPress
- **Website:** funeralpress.org
- **Description:** Ghana's all-in-one funeral design platform — premium funeral brochures, posters, invitations, booklets, and online memorial pages with an elegant editor, downloadable as print-ready PDFs.
- **Tagline:** A dignified farewell, beautifully made.
- **Brand colors:** `#0A0A0A` (obsidian, primary) · `#D4AF37` (gold, accent) · `#FAFAFA` (ivory) · `#1A1A1A` (charcoal) · `#FEFCF8` (cream)
- **Fonts:** Playfair Display (headings) · Inter / system sans (body)
- **Tone of voice:** Dignified, warm, premium, reverent. A *celebration of life*, never morbid or clinical. Speaks to grieving Ghanaian families with empathy and quiet elegance.
- **Audience:** Families in Ghana planning funerals and memorials; churches and funeral organizers.
- **Proof points:** 500+ families served · 2,000+ tributes created · loved across Ghana.

## Notes & limits

- **Imagery is for mood/hero use.** On-image text is decorative/garbled (the generation-model ceiling) — add real names, dates, and details in the FuneralPress editor or Canva.
- **Verticals** were reframed from the square hero shots (center-fill crop to 9:16); backgrounds are obsidian-black so the crop is seamless. The phone shots leave black headroom on purpose — drop a CTA + logo there.
- **How they were generated:** Cloudflare Workers AI (Flux) via a temporary worker (since deleted), art-directed in the Obsidian & Gold house style. To regenerate or add products, lead every image prompt with: *"Cinematic premium product photography, celebration-of-life mood, dignified serene and warm. Deep obsidian black background with a single soft warm gold key light, ivory accents, subtle gold-foil detail, tasteful Ghanaian elegance, white lilies. Shallow depth of field, editorial 8k, reverent and luxurious, not morbid."*
- **Caching:** R2 objects serve with `immutable` 1-year cache. To replace an image, upload under a new filename (e.g. `-v2`) rather than overwriting, or edge caches may serve the old version.
